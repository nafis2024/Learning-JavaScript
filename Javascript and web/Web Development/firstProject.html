Coffee Arabica

Coffea arabica, also known as the Arabica coffee, is a species of flowering plant in the coffee and madder family Rubiaceae.
Scientific name: Coffea arabica
Kingdom: Plantae
Order: Gentianales

Robusta Coffee

Robusta Coffee is coffee made from the beans of the Coffea canephora plant.
Scientific name: Coffea canephora
Kingdom: Plantae
Order: Gentianales

Americano Coffee

An americano is just water and espresso.
Scientific name: Caffe Americano
Kingdom: Plantae
Order: Gentianales
